How to Play

This is Pictionary - a drawing guessing game. The game goes like this:

  • 1. The first user to connect will be set as the drawer. Everyone else will be a guesser.
  • 2. The drawer will see a word and try to get the guessers to figure out what the word is.
  • 3. Once a user guesses the correct answer, the drawer can click on their name to make them the new drawer!
  • 4. The drawer can clear the canvas at any time if needed.
  • 5. Every time a correct guess is chosen, that user gets 10 points! Once you get 50 points you switch to blindfold mode (you can't see what you draw, but your guessers can!) for extra challenge.
